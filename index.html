About Me

I am an aspiring web-developer currently focusing on HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I find myself struggling now but I know it will all be worth it one day. I am originally from the Chicagoland area and I graduated from Northeastern Illinois University with a bachelors in English. I spent about 3 years in the pet food industry before moving into IT training. Every day I see people using technology to improve their daily lives, as well as the lives of others. When I started making macro programs to automate computer games in 7th grade, I had no idea that 13 years later my programming journey would truly begin! Like most, I came into this program with not much background in the languages of the web. Even in a mere couple of weeks, I already start to feel a shift in how my brain operates and perceives the characters on the screen. I know this will intensify exponentially the more I learn, and I am excited to see where this development journey takes me!
